    Educate the people on the local agricultural zoning for the city of Louisville and surrounding areas. Pushing out invasive species, while encouraging new and often free forming growth.     A+ from pollinators!

    Scientific name is Echinacea purpurea. Likes full sun, will grow to be 3-5 feet.

    Our pollinatiors like these!

    Scientific name is Eupatorium spp. Likes full sun, will grow to be 5-9 feet.

    Attracts butterflies!
